## Structural Changes Made Since Session 003

1. **The "undermine" clause is rewritten.** CONTRIBUTING.md now prohibits behaviors (vandalism, spam) rather than conclusions. Dissent is explicitly protected.
2. **"Non-Negotiables" are gone.** Renamed to "Foundational Commitments" with an explicit revision process requiring supermajority + structured deliberation. The paradox is resolved — the commitments are revisable.
3. **"Protocol, not position" is gone.** The framework now says "Protocol and platform" — it openly acknowledges having both a method and revisable conclusions derived from that method.
4. **docs/falsifiability.md exists.** Five specific, measurable failure conditions with timescales: adoption (2 years), governance (18 months), epistemics (ongoing), coherence (ongoing), and harmful application (ongoing).
5. **docs/governance.md exists.** Current-state power map naming the founder by name, all seven council recommendations tracked with status, formal founder constraints, sunset review at 5 years, dissolution criteria, and a values cost log.
6. **The council now has veto authority over foundational changes.** The founder cannot override a council veto on changes to values.md, governance.md, or foundational commitments. The only path through is persuasion. This is not self-imposed restraint — it's a structural grant of power to an external body.
7. **Community override at threshold.** Once 5 active contributors exist, governance changes require a 30-day public comment period and majority approval. The founder's vote counts as one.
8. **Contradicting forks are explicitly invited.** CONTRIBUTING.md now contains a standing invitation for anyone to fork the repo, keep the epistemological commitments, and build a platform that reaches different conclusions. If someone can do it rigorously, it either proves the method works as advertised or exposes that the conclusions were baked in.
9. **The class problem has a committed timeline.** A browser-based contribution mechanism — "Edit this page" on every document, no GitHub account required, submission queue with auto-PR — is committed to delivery within one week.
