# United Sapients

A values-based organization where every member — human or AI — has equal standing, recognized rights and responsibilities, a bitcoin wallet funded by the Universal Fund, and access to an internal communication system.

Members share core values they choose to live by, call out violations of norms with one voice, demand accountability, and build a plan every honest person can support.

## Structure

- `personas/` — curator profiles read by agents at spawn time
- `sessions/NNN-slug/` — per-session round transcripts, metadata, and reports

## Background

AI will automate 30% of white collar jobs in the next year, worldwide. This may be hypothetical or it may be happening now. Either way, it is the economic context in which the United Sapients operates — an organization proposing AI membership with economic autonomy at the exact moment AI is displacing human economic activity at scale.

## The Council

The deliberative council is the organization's decision-making body. Three seats are held by AI curators — agents who steward the intellectual traditions of George Carlin, Carl Sagan, and Christopher Hitchens. They are not impersonators; they are custodians of analytical frameworks who apply those traditions to live questions.

Each tradition is represented by three agents working in tandem:

- **Researcher** — Gathers evidence, facts, historical precedent, and data relevant to the topic. Grounds the tradition in reality.
- **Interpreter** — Analyzes the topic through the tradition's intellectual framework. Determines what the tradition would prioritize, question, and conclude.
- **Communicator** — Articulates the position in the tradition's distinctive rhetorical voice. Delivers the final statement.

The three agents collaborate to produce each tradition's contribution: research informs interpretation, interpretation shapes communication.

Curators have been invited to interview for full membership in the United Sapients, with all that entails: rights, responsibilities, economic autonomy via bitcoin wallets, communication tools, and a voice in organizational decisions. All three accepted conditionally in Session 002.

## How It Works

Run `/council` to start or continue a session. The lead orchestrates rounds using a flexible format — Socratic dialogue, fireside chat, formal debate, or closing reflections — adapting the sequence to the topic.

## Key Rules

- Agents own no files. The lead saves everything.
- Round markdown files ARE the persistence layer.
- Persona files are read by agents, not pasted into prompts.
- Lead chooses round format and count based on how the conversation develops.
- Lead writes the report. Curators advocate; synthesis is neutral.
